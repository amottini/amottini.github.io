About Me

I am an experienced AI and Machine Learning professional with over a decade of experience building cutting-edge AI products and production systems. My expertise spans areas such as language models, recommender systems, and multi-modal / graph-based models, with a particular focus on combining cutting-edge approaches with practical business impact.

I am currently a Sr. Applied Scientist in Amazon's Stores Foundational AI organization, where I lead initiatives focused on developing LLM-powered generative and representation (embedding) models applied across Amazon to achieve step-function improvement on applications used by millions of users. In this role, I have also demonstrated leadership in managing and scaling AI teams, overseeing projects from conception to production.

My journey at Amazon began with Alexa AI, where I worked in multiple teams including Alexa Personalization and Alexa Data Services. In these roles, I led the development and deployment of machine learning solutions for personalizing the Alexa experience, as well as novel approaches to data labeling that significantly reduced costs while preserving user privacy.

Before Amazon, I was a Data Scientist at the Innovation and Research Team in the Amadeus IT Group . My main activities included conceptualizing new research ideas, developing prototypes and performing data analysis for applications in the travel industry. I also I have received two Master degrees, a M.Sc. in Computational Biology and Biomedicine from the University of Nice Sophia Antipolis (France) and an Electrical Engineering Degree from the Universidad de la Republica (Uruguay).

Throughout my career, I've maintained a builder's mindset, focusing on solving complex business challenges by combining established methods with innovative approaches. I thrive on turning cutting-edge research into production-ready systems that deliver measurable business impact.
